About Us

JahNewEnterprizes was founded in 2024 by Jah New, a passionate and talented chef who wanted to share his skills and vision with the world. We gathered a team of like-minded and experienced chefs who shared a similiar passion for creativity and excellence. Together, they have worked on various projects for clients from different industries and backgrounds, delivering stunning and unique cuisine that meet their needs and expectations.
